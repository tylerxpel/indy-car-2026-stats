Key Takeaways

Based on filtered campaign data
✓ Strength

Exceptional lead capture — 96.3%

Nearly everyone who started the quiz became a lead. The prize incentive (Full Vehicle Tint Install) contributed to this, but the quiz format itself — low friction, fast, mobile-native — is the delivery mechanism that made it work. A prize alone doesn't drive 91% completion; the experience does.

✓ Strength

89% of completers scored "Well done"

207 of 233 respondents who reached the results screen achieved the high-score result. This speaks to generally strong brand and product awareness — but the question-level data reveals specific gaps around the warranty duration and full product lineup that the aggregate score obscures.

⚠ Opportunity

Warranty knowledge is the biggest gap

34% of respondents didn't know XPEL PPF carries a 10-year warranty, with 29% guessing "5 years." This is one of XPEL's strongest competitive differentiators and appears to be under-communicated. See the Question Breakdown for the full breakdown.

⚠ Opportunity

24% don't know XPEL's full product range

On the "what products does XPEL produce?" question, 24% selected a single product category rather than "All of the Above." Customers siloed into PPF-only or tint-only awareness represent a direct cross-sell opportunity through targeted follow-up content.

→ Audience Signal

iPhone-first, not just mobile-first

70.9% iOS, 28.2% Android, essentially 0% desktop. All future campaigns, tools, and landing pages targeting this dealer/installer audience should be designed for iOS Safari specifically before any other environment.

⚠ Opportunity

All traffic came from a single send window

317 of 340 views arrived June 19–21 with near-zero activity by June 22. No organic traffic at all. Embedding the link in dealer portal nav, onboarding emails, or recurring nurture sequences would extend the quiz's shelf life well beyond a single campaign blast.
